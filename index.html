Delicious Banana Chips Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Peel the bananas and slice them thinly.
  2. Place the banana slices in a bowl and add the lemon juice. Toss gently to coat the slices.
  3. Spread the banana slices on a baking sheet and sprinkle them with salt.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the banana slices in batches until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Remove the banana chips from the pan and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. Allow the banana chips to cool before serving.
